About CropCart:
CropCart is a platform dedicated to revolutionizing the agricultural market by connecting farmers and buyers seamlessly. Our mission is to empower local farmers, reduce middlemen, and ensure fair pricing for high-quality produce.

Developed as a final year project by Deepank Yadav , Manraj Singh, Purab Pareek at Jecrc University, CropCart combines technology and innovation to address real-world challenges in the agricultural industry.
